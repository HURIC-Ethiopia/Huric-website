About HURIC

Hawassa University Robotics Club (HURIC) is a student-led innovation community dedicated to advancing robotics, artificial intelligence, and engineering excellence.

We empower students with practical knowledge, teamwork, and competitive experience, preparing them to participate in national robotics competitions and technology events.

Our Mission

Develop innovative thinkers and skilled robotics engineers.

Our Vision

Become a leading university robotics club in Ethiopia.

Our Goal

Compete and win national robotics competitions.
